Privacy Policy – HLN Abonnement Blocker

This privacy policy describes how the "HLN Abonnement Blocker" Chrome extension (hereinafter "the extension") handles your data.

Overview

The extension is designed to toggle (via a switch) a blocker for subscription popups on HLN.be. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data.

Data Collection & Usage

• No Data Collected: The extension does not collect or store any user data. It only uses Chrome's "storage" permission (via chrome.storage.sync) to save your toggle preference (enabled or disabled) locally on your device.

• No Third-Party Services: The extension does not communicate with any third-party servers or services.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage – Used to save your toggle preference (enabled or disabled) locally.
  • scripting – Used (if needed) to inject or remove a content script on HLN.be.
  • activeTab – Used to reload the current tab (on HLN.be) when you toggle the blocker.

Additionally, the extension declares a "host_permission" (https://www.hln.be/*) so that it can run its content script on HLN.be.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the author (Robin) via the extension's homepage or GitHub repository.
